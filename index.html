Workflow

Workflow is a tool that lets you digitise your processes.
With it you can design, manage and track workflow tasks digitally.

Pipes

Each workflow process is called a pipe.  Pipes let you create and design a process and make them digital. A pipe is made up of Stages and Cards.

Learn More

Steps

Each workflow has a series of steps. Tasks arrive as Cards and move from left to right along the Workflow Steps. You can add, remove and edit these stages to do different things.

Learn More

Cards

Each task is created as a card. You can assign people, create checklists, leave comments and move tasks to different stages. Each card is fully customisable.
